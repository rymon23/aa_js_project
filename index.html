Welcome to AI Fitness Dash

AI Fitness Dash is a machine learning visualization that utilizes a genetic algorithm to simulate natural selection. A group of AI bots are initially generated with a randomized set of genes, each corresponding to a different characteristic or environmental response.

The fittest bots, those that get closest to the other side of a dynamically generated map, are then selected and cross-bred to create the next generation. With the right configuration, each generation will become more effective at navigating across the map.
